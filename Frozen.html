Frozen 2
PG 1h 44min 2019 Adventure, Animation, Kids & Family Why was Elsa born with magical powers? What truths about the past await Elsa as she ventures into the unknown to the enchanted forests and dark seas beyond Arendelle? The answers are calling her but also threatening her kingdom.
